Spaghetti Carbonara

Italian
Pasta
Quick & Easy
30 min

Description

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. This creamy and delicious recipe is perfect for a quick weeknight dinner and uses simple ingredients to create an authentic Roman carbonara.

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt for pasta water

Instructions

1

Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook the spaghetti according to package directions until al dente.

2

While the pasta cooks, fry the pancetta in a large skillet over medium heat until crispy, about 5-7 minutes.

3

In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, Pecorino Romano, Parmesan, and plenty of black pepper.

4

Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining. Drain the spaghetti and add it to the skillet with pancetta off the heat.

5

Quickly pour the egg mixture over the hot pasta, tossing constantly. Add reserved pasta water little by little to create a creamy sauce. Serve immediately with extra cheese and pepper.
