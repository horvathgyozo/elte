Pasta Carbonara

Italian
30 min
4 servings
Medium

John Doe

Posted on Jan 15, 2026

A classic Italian pasta dish made with eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. This authentic recipe creates a creamy sauce without using cream!

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 200g pancetta or guanciale, diced
  • 4 large eggs
  • 100g Pecorino Romano cheese, grated
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated
  • Black pepper, freshly ground
  • Salt for pasta water

Instructions

  1. Prepare the sauce: In a bowl, whisk together eggs, Pecorino, Parmesan, and a generous amount of black pepper.
  2. Cook the pasta: Bring a large pot of salted water to boil. Cook spaghetti according to package directions until al dente.
  3. Cook the pancetta: While pasta cooks, sauté pancetta in a large pan over medium heat until crispy (about 5-7 minutes).
  4. Combine: Reserve 1 cup pasta water, then drain pasta. Add hot pasta to the pan with pancetta. Remove from heat.
  5. Create the sauce: Quickly stir in the egg mixture, adding pasta water gradually to create a creamy sauce. The residual heat will cook the eggs.
  6. Serve: Serve immediately with extra cheese and black pepper on top.
